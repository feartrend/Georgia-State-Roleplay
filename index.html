Our Mission

Serious Roleplay, Real Standards

Georgia State Roleplay is built around one idea — that great roleplay comes from real structure. We hold our officers to genuine law enforcement standards, follow realistic procedures, and foster a community where every call, every stop, and every pursuit feels earned.

Whether you're a trooper with GSP, an officer with DVPD, or brand new to the community, you'll find a place here if you're willing to put in the work and respect the craft.

To provide an immersive, professional, and fair law enforcement roleplay environment where integrity, realism, and community come first.

— GSR Mission Statement
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

⚖️
Integrity
We hold ourselves to the same standards on and off duty. Honesty and accountability are non-negotiable in every interaction.
🎯
Realism
Procedures exist for a reason. We follow real SOPs, use realistic tactics, and treat every scenario with the gravity it deserves.
🤝
Respect
Every member of this community — civilian or officer — deserves to be treated with dignity. We lead by example.
📈
Growth
We invest in our officers. Training, mentorship, and feedback are how we get better together as a community.
Active Agencies

Who We Are

Georgia State Patrol
GSP

The primary statewide law enforcement agency. GSP handles highway patrol, pursuits, and holds exclusive authorization for PITs and Field Sobriety Testing.

Douglasville Police Department
DVPD

The local municipal police force covering the Douglasville area. DVPD handles patrol, calls for service, and local law enforcement operations.

Cobb County Sheriff's Office
CCSO

A limited sub-unit operating under DVPD's chain of command. Cobb County runs a small number of units and follows all DVPD SOPs.
